Registration for the 2019 Summer APC DESC meeting

The registration fee for the collaboration meeting is 228€ (114€ for students), which covers attendance to the 5-day meeting, a welcoming cocktail, lunches, and coffee breaks. Fees do not cover the Collaboration Meeting Dinner on Thursday July 18, 2019 at the Musée d'Orsay. A extra fee of 48€ (15€ for students) will be required if you want to attend this dinner.

The local organizing committee also offers accommodation for students at a special price (204€ for 6 nights from the 14th to the 20th of July, 2019) located at the Cité Universitaire de Paris (subject to availability, on a first come, first served basis).
